Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is this service?

It’s a web tool that automatically generates personalized M3U playlists using your credentials (DNS, username/password, or M3U URL), eliminating the need for manual edits.

❓ How does it work?

  1. Browse and select the desired service.
  2. Choose Automatic or Manual process in the modal.
  3. Select M3U or Xtream.
  4. Enter your credentials.
  5. Click Generate.
  6. Playlist downloads and is shown with EPG and GitHub URL.

❓ Google Drive M3U URL

You can generate a direct M3U URL hosted on Google Drive — no downloads needed.

  1. Select Google Drive before generating.
  2. Authorize access.
  3. You get a direct M3U URL.

❓ Problems with Google Drive?

Try a different browser and disable pop-up blockers. Avoid 2FA accounts if errors persist.

❓ Is it safe?

Yes, no credentials are stored. The code is open source and hosted securely.

❓ Can I share my list?

Yes, you can publish a new list or update an existing one with a password. Moderators review submissions.

❓ Why does the EPG say “No information”?

EPG sync can take up to 30 minutes. Try using a VPN if issues persist.

❓ Why is the service slow?

The tool runs on a small server and may be slow at peak hours.
